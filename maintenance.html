Physical Cleaning Tips

  • Use compressed air to blow out dust (do this outside!)
  • Clean fans and heatsinks - dust buildup causes overheating
  • Wipe down surfaces with microfiber cloth
  • Check cable management - tangled cables block airflow
  • Replace thermal paste every 2-3 years (or if temps are high)
