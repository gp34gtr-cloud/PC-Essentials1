Common Mistakes to Avoid

Learn from others' mistakes before you build

🔌

Forgetting I/O Shield

Install the I/O shield BEFORE putting the motherboard in the case. You'll have to take everything out if you forget.

🧲

Static Electricity

Touch your case or use an anti-static wristband before handling components.

💨

CPU Cooler Plastic

Remove the protective plastic from the CPU cooler before mounting. This is the #1 beginner mistake.

🔋

PSU Cables

Only use cables that came with YOUR PSU. Mixing cables from different PSUs can fry your components.

🎮

Display Cable Location

Connect your monitor to the GPU, not the motherboard. Otherwise you'll use integrated graphics.

🌡️

Thermal Paste Amount

A pea-sized amount in the center is enough. Too much can actually hurt cooling performance.
